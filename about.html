Wuruwuru is a collection of random publishing experiments, each one made with a different set of friends and freelancers.

I'm doing this for three reasons:

First, to produce high quality content like the ones I consume e.g. The Pudding, Psyche and Adult Swim. I've followed them for many years and they do such good work.

Secondly, to work with freelancers and document my experience of the Nigerian creative industry.

Finally, I get to both participate in and support independent creation, which I really believe in.

I called it Wuruwuru because it's a trial and error approach to publishing, but hopefully we make some great stories. Enjoy!
