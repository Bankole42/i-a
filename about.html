Wuruwuru is a collection of random publishing experiments, each one made with a different set of friends and freelancers.

I'm doing this for three reasons:

First, I'm learning to make stories as good as the publications I admire e.g. The Pudding, Psyche and Adult Swim. I've followed them for many years and they do such good work.

Secondly, I get to work with peers outside of my job, learn about their process / teach them mine, find out how much things costs and understand how the industry works.

Finally, I'm hoping to contribute to documentation about creative work in Nigeria so I'm journaling and sharing everything openly.

I hope you enjoy the experiment.
