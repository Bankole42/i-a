Wuruwuru is a self publication. We make multimedia content like visual essays, datasets and animated videos e.g. this database of album covers or this illustrated guide to rolling a joint.

The goal is to tell obscure but fascinating stories about design, music and internet culture in Nigeria. We want to make exciting interactive stories, inspired by projects like The Pudding and Adult Swim, to name a few.

The stories are done in collaboration with other independent creators and there's no cadence for publishing. We're taking it one at a time and documenting on Medium.

I hope you enjoy the experiment.
